Lost something at BRACU?

Don't worry. FoundIT is your central hub for reporting lost and finding found items on campus.

How It Works

1. Report Lost Item

Fill out a simple form with details about your lost item. The more details, the better!

2. Search Found Items

Browse our database of items found on campus. You can filter by category, location, and date.

3. Reclaim Your Item

Found a match? Follow the instructions to verify ownership and coordinate a pickup.
